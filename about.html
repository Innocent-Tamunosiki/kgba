About KGDA

Objective

The key objectives of the KGDA are as follows:
  1. To reawaken interest and active participation of all stakeholders in the development of Kalabari community towards economic wellbeing of the people!
  2. To provide enabling environment for the development of traditional small scale enterprises in Kalabari communities!
  3. To promote activities that will lead to the sustainable development of the Kalabari kingdom!
